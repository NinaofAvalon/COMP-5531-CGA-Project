Welcome to CrsMgr Group-work Assistant(CGA)

This webpage allows you to set up and update courses, course sections, course instructors and TAs, and student groups.
The web page also allows you to post notices that will be visible to all current users of CGA.
The features offered as menus are explained below.

Features

  • Courses and Course Sections Information
    • List the contact information for the instructor and tutors for the course.
    • Insert instructor and tutors for the course.
    • Update instructor and tutors for the course.
  • Instructor and Tutors Information
    • List the contact information for the instructor and tutors for the course.
    • Insert instructor and tutors for the course.
    • Update instructor and tutors for the course.
  • Students
    • View detailed information of all students in the section.
    • Insert set of students.
  • Course Groups
    • View detailed information of all section groups.
    • Insert groups in the section.
    • Make changes to teammates in groups over the term.
  • Notices

Post notices visible to all users of the CGA system.

  • Change Password

Change your password for access to the system.

  • Change Username

Update your username which is recorded in the system.
